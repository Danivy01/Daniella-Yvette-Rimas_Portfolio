Cyclistance Application

Road Assistance for Cyclist is within few clicks and the closest available help will arrive at your exact location

Project Overview

Cyclistance is an Android Application that is our Capstone Project in College that giving assistance to cyclist during emergency situations. Cyclistance was conceptualized in response to the evolving transportation landscape following the COVID-19 pandemic, which saw a surge in cycling as an alternative to traditional transportation modes due to increased health risks and transportation costs. This shift highlighted the need for improved cycling infrastructure and more informed transportation planning, particularly in light of a rise in cycling for both exercise and travel.

The app connects nearby cyclists through key features like distress signals and real-time communication, fostering a sense of community among users. The impact is significant, leading to faster response times, the formation of local communities, and improved overall safety and confidence for cyclists on the road. Cyclistance sets a new standard for cyclist support, offering a quick and accessible way for users to seek help with just a tap.

My Role

In my capacity as the UI/UX Designer within the team, my primary responsibilities encompassed the creation of visually compelling designs, the development of interactive prototypes, and the formulation of wireframes. My role extended to ensuring the seamless alignment of these designs with both technical requirements and overarching team objectives. Collaboration with our Lead Developer was an integral aspect of my responsibilities, as we worked closely together to integrate design elements effectively within the project framework. This collaborative approach allowed for the harmonization of design aesthetics with the team's technical requirements and overarching goals, contributing to the overall success of the project.
