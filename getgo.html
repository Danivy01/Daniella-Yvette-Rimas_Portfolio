GetGo Website

Designed effectively to allow admins to oversee and manage lending operations using the intuitive features of the user-friendly website.

Project Overview

The GetGo Website has been specifically conceptualized for Lend2GetGo, a prominent lending company. Previously, the company relied on a generic online platform for its transactions; however, with the development of the GetGo Website, each of its branches now enjoys a dedicated interface. Lend2GetGo, a valued client of our team, entrusted us with the creation of this website. It exclusively caters to administrative functions within the company, while customers access services through the dedicated GetGo Application platform.

My Role

As a UI/UX Designer within the team, I actively collaborated and made significant contributions to the cross-functional team's efforts in crafting our client's website. My responsibilities encompassed designing and prototyping the website to ensure optimal user experience and interface functionality.
